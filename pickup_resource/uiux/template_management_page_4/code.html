Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe in offline mode? chevron_right

Yes, absolutely. Your pickup codes are stored using industry-standard AES-256 encryption directly on your device. When in offline mode, no data leaves your phone, ensuring complete privacy from external servers while still giving you access to your stored vault.

How do I share a code with my group? chevron_right

Sharing is built into the core experience. Tap on any active pickup code, select the 'Group Share' icon, and choose the contact or group you'd like to invite. They will receive a secure temporary access link that syncs automatically with their app.

Can I sync codes across multiple devices? chevron_right

Yes, if you have Cloud Sync enabled in Settings. This allows your codes to be available on any device where you are logged into your account.

What happens when a code expires? chevron_right

Expired codes are moved to your 'Archive' folder automatically. You can still view them for record-keeping, but they will no longer appear on your Lock Screen Live Activity.
